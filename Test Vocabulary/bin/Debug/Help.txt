Tutorial:
1. Click  on button " Run " to load data.
2. Write your answer and click on button " Submit " to test your answer.
3. If you want to add your new vocabullary. You must click on button " Add vocab " to add new vocabulary.
4. Click on button " My vocabulary ", you can see a list of your vocabulary.
5. Click on button " Hint " to get answer.
6. If you want to change your data, please! come to " bin\Debug\Data.txt" to change data.
